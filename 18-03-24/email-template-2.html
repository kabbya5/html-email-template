We will learn about Vocation today

The word “vocation” means “a call”. That call is like a little inner voice that very often reminds you what you really like to do. If you listen and pay attention, you will enjoy whatever job you choose to do in life!

Sometimes we find our vocation through a subject that we truly enjoy at school, Or, we watch someone do something and think “that’s what I want to do when I grow up”. You can also discover your vocation through your plays or your imagination. Have you found your vocation? Good! If you know, start to practice; if not, try “listening hard” to discover your little inner voice.
